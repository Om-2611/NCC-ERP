📊 AI-Based ERP System for NCC Cadets

Alert System

AI-powered alert system to prevent last-minute reporting confusion.

  • CO/Clerk uploads camp details and reporting dates.
  • LLM analyzes if the reporting date is close to the publication date.
  • If date is too close, ERP flags it as "last-minute notice."
  • System identifies eligible cadets based on ERP profiles.
  • Personalized WhatsApp alerts are sent instantly.

⏰ Smart Camp Reporting

Scheduled Notifications & Response Tracking

Ensures cadets are notified even if the notice is early.

  • If reporting date is >3 days away, system schedules auto-alert 3 days before camp.
  • Personalized messages sent via WhatsApp.
  • Cadets can acknowledge via reply or link.
  • ERP tracks confirmations and updates CO/ANO dashboard.

🧑‍💻 Smart Camp Reporting

Cadet Acknowledgment Tracking

Real-time visibility for COs and ANOs.

  • Dashboard shows who received and responded.
  • Risk alerts if many cadets haven't responded.
  • Helps plan replacements in advance.
  • Reduces last-minute no-shows.

🗂️ Centralized Nominal Roll Workflow

Stepwise Process

Digital forwarding of selections from college to battalion.

  • Directorate allocates camp vacancy to Groups.
  • Groups assign vacancies to respective battalions.
  • Each battalion assigns sub-vacancies to ANOs via ERP.
  • ANOs select cadets from their institution through the portal.
  • Clerk & CO review and digitally sign the BN list.
  • Forwarded to Group Clerk.

🔁 Nominal Roll Finalization

Hierarchical digital approval with traceability

  • Group Clerk compiles final list.
  • Group CO digitally signs and sends to Directorate.
  • Directorate prepares final list for the hosting BN.
  • Final signed nominal roll sent to organizing BN Clerk & CO.
  • Full audit trail across all levels.

🎖️ Certificate Verification & Storage

LLM ensures only authentic certificates are accepted

  • Cadet uploads post-camp certificate.
  • AI validates name, camp type, and structure.
  • If approved, certificate is auto-uploaded to Google Drive.
  • Rejected certificates get instant feedback for correction.
  • All approved certificates are accessible by cadet & ANO.

📢 Motivation Panel

Notification System

Highlight achievements to inspire cadets.

  • ERP publishes selected cadet names publicly.
  • Details include camp name, unit, and college.
  • COs can add motivational remarks.
  • Builds competition and morale within units.
